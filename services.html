Services

What I
offer

As a director, director of photography, and camera operator, he is involved in every stage of your project. His expertise goes from the concept to the shoot to all the way through to post-production.

As a director, he transforms concepts into cinematic realities, bringing technical and artistic rigor to give each project a unique visual identity.

Believing that every decision must serve the storytelling, he gets involved right from pre-production to define the artistic direction.

  • Visual and artistic direction
  • Lighting plan and reference boards
  • Camera and lens selection
  • Directing the electrical and grip team
  • Colour grade supervision
  • Fiction, documentary, advertising, music video

Available as a DOP or a camera operator on your sets. He works closely with the director to give a strong visual identity to every frame, bringing technical rigour and sensitivity to the shot.

Comfortable in varied conditions, he adapts to any shooting style to best serve the director's vision.

  • Solo or crew shooting
  • Commercial, documentary, events
  • Available in France and internationally
