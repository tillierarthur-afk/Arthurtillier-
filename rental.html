A camera built for
extreme slow motion

The Pixboom Sparks is one of the few cameras capable of combining a large-format Super 35mm sensor with true ultra slow-motion capture — all in a compact, field-ready body.

With a global shutter that eliminates rolling shutter artefacts, 13 stops of dynamic range, and dual native ISO, it handles both controlled studio environments and demanding outdoor shoots.

Available with a full set of E-mount and PL-mount adapters, and 2 × 2.5 TB SSDs for long shooting days.

Sensor Super 35mm — 4.6K
Shutter Global shutter
Dynamic range 13 stops
ISO Dual native — 400 / 1600
Frame rates 4.6K up to 1025 fps
2K up to 2182 fps
